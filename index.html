Blast is back!
Black Stones, also known as Blast (ブラスト) is a Japanese punk band from the late 90's. Originating in the small coastal town of Meguro, they spiraled into popularity, and re-established their band in the city of Tokyo. There, the band made their debut single with the hit song "Blast!" on October 31st, 2001. The current 4 active members are: Nana Osaki, Nobu Terashia, Shinichi Okazaki, and Yasushi Takagi.
