Decelis Academy
Decelis Academy is located in the heart of Riverland, a booming city with festivities. With an extreme desire for knowledge and history, our academy is home to hundreds of scholars. Decelis Academy is a rigorous learning environment with a closed off campus life containing everything you will ever need. School life is accompanied with a large and cozy dormitory.

Decelis students are required to wear uniforms and adhere to dress code.
A grand library of knowledge and history is accessible to all students.
